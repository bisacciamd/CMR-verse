CMR publications 2019 to 2024
A quantitative appraisal
  • Methods
  • Journals
  • Papers
  • Authors
  • Systematic search of all CMR-related studies indexed on PubMed as of 8th August 2024
  • Journal search restricted to top 10% journals in cardiology and radiology on the Scopus database
  • Author search restricted to first and last authors with at least 2 CMR-related publications.
  • A total of 24 journals in the Cardiology and Cardiovascular Medicine category, and 24 journals in the Radiology, Nuclear Medicine and Imaging category were identified.
  • 20 journals had articles related to CMR.
  • Between 2019 and 2024, a total of 3144 CMR-related papers were published. Of these, 332 (%) were published in top 10% journals by a total of 297 unique first authors and 256 unique last authors.
